Intel

Intel is a global leader in semiconductor manufacturing, known primarily for its CPUs, including the Intel Core, Xeon, and Pentium series. Recently, Intel has expanded into GPUs with the Intel Arc series. The company's processors are found in millions of PCs worldwide, providing robust performance for both casual users and professionals.
